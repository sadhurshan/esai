# Project Overview — Elements Supply AI
## Mission
Unify engineering docs, digital twins, sourcing (RFQ/RFP → Quotes → PO → Orders), quality, invoicing, supplier risk, and ESG into one audit‑ready workspace.

## Target Users
- Buyer Admin / Requester — RFQs, quotes, POs, orders, docs.
- Supplier Admin / Estimator — capabilities, RFQs, quotes, POs.
- Finance — invoices (tracking), reconciliation.
- Platform Admin — onboarding, usage & plans, audit, announcements.

## Core Modules
1) Document Control Hub • 2) Digital Twin • 3) Maintenance Library • 4) Sourcing & RFQ/RFP • 5) POs & Orders • 6) Receiving & Quality • 7) Invoicing (tracking) • 8) Supplier Risk & ESG • 9) Analytics & Copilot • 10) Admin Console

## What’s Out (Phase‑1)
No online payments, no SSO, no blockchain supplier auth.
