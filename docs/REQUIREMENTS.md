# Functional Requirements (Distilled)
Use this as Copilot's source of truth. See PDF for detail.

- **Supplier Discovery**: rich filters/sorts; verified profiles; AI match with rationale.
- **RFQ/RFP**: CAD‑aware fields; Open Bidding; AI prefill + validation.
- **Quotes**: unified inbox; price/lead time/attachments; AI suggestions; revisions/withdrawals.
- **Compare & Award**: sortable columns; split awards.
- **POs**: auto/manual with approvals; dual‑brand PDFs; acknowledgements & change orders.
- **Orders**: statuses + timeline; tracking number.
- **Receiving & Quality**: GRNs, inspections, NCRs.
- **Invoices (tracking)**: generate from PO; status only.
- **Docs & CAD**: versioned, watermarked, audit; secure sharing.
- **Analytics**: cycle times, OTIF, win/loss, spend, forecast accuracy; CSV/PDF export.
- **Integrations**: CSV now; ERP/CMMS later via API/webhooks.
- **RBAC**: least privilege across buyer/supplier/finance/platform roles.
- **Company & Users**: signup approval, invites, multi‑org, domain verify, audit.
- **Billing & Entitlements**: Stripe Cashier; Starter/Growth/Enterprise; gating + grace.
- **Approvals Matrix**: up to 5 levels; delegations; full audit.
- **Global Search**: saved searches; tenant scoped.
- **Notifications**: push + email + digests; prefs.
- **Export/Backup**: tenant export; nightly backups (30 days).
- **Localization/Units**: i18n JSON; metric/imperial; currency formatting.
- **Audit UI**: filterable + diff modal.
- **Must‑Adds**: KYC, clarifications & amendments, lifecycle deadlines, multi‑currency & taxes/incoterms, three‑way match, custom PDF templates, bulk CSV imports.

See `/docs/DEFINITION_OF_DONE.md` and `/docs/CONVENTIONS.md` for acceptance and guard rails.
